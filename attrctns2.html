Mahabir Temple is situated in kuraich Sasaram. It is one of the ancient temple also known by Kuraich Temple, which is situated on the Sasaram Aara (Nokha) road. This temple is related to the Maharaja of Nokha who had constructed this temple. The people of entire Rohtas come to visit this temple. It is believed that in order to please Lord Indra at the time of rain in the rainy season, rotate is arranged on Tuesday and Saturdo of Adara in the Kuraich Mahavir temple of Sasaram. Every Tuesday and Saturdays of the Adara Nakshatra, the devotees coming from far and wide in this city are busy with thier work and stress.
