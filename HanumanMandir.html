Hanuman Mandir
Pic 1: Hanuman Mandir

Hanuman Mandir in Dehri On Sone, Rohtas is one of the top Temples in Dehri On Sone. It is located in Dehri Bazar near Kamdhenu Sweets. It is one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Hanuman. There is lack of vehicle parking near temple which leds to termendous traffic jams and little crowded place for temple due to shops all around the area.

This temple is consist of 3 floors: Ground floor, Middle floor & Top floor. Beautiful idol of hanuman ji is situated on ground floor of temple. In Middle floor, idols of Lord Vishnu & Goddess Lakshmi is situated. On the top most floor of this temple the divine family of Lord Shiva idols is situated: Shivling with Nandi Gai(Bull) , Idols of Maa Parvati & Lord Ganesha.

Pic 2: Idol of Hanuman Ji

On Tuesday and Saturday temple is filled with devotees because this two days is considered very auspicious day to worship Lord Hanuman. Devotees offer Moong dal Laddoo as prasad to 'Hanuman ji' which they bought from nearby shops. This Laddoo is very delicious and famous among the citizens of Dehri-On-Sone. Every Saturday 'Khichdi' is distributed as prasad in this temple.
