Durga Maa Mandir is situated in Taxi Stand Road of Bikramganj in Rohtas District. It is also known as Durga Asthan. The locality in which Durga Mandir is located is known as Punjabi Muhala. On the ocassion of Ramjanmotsav, Bhabhya Bhandara is organized here. In this bhandara thousands of devotees took part in Mata Rani's courtyard. Bhabya Bhandara is organized from Durga Puja Samiti station. Bhabya Bhandara is offered after the completion of the Ramcharit Manas narrative and the completion of the Ramjanmotsav.

Durga Maa Mandir(Bikramganj)

Mahabir Mandir Charanpuri is situated in Nasirganj of Rohtas District. This temple is located at Nasriganj - Daudnagar Road. It is one of the holiest Hindu temples in Nasriganj dedicated to Lord Hanuman. This place is popular due to beautiful idol of Bajrang bali. Devotees of Hanuman ji of Nasriganj comes here specially on Tuesday and Saturday. Each year on the ocassion of Ram Navmi, Ram Navmi Shobha Yotra Aayojan is organized here and also bhavya Maha Aarti is organized by Bajrang Dal.

Mahabir Mandir Charanpuri

Panch-Mandir is situated in Nasriganj near Nasirganj Police Station in Rohtas District. This place is popular due to beautiful idol of Shri Ram , Laxman & Sita ji. Devotees comes here to worship always organized Havan. It is one of the ancient temple of Nasriganj. It is also said that the God Rama had passed from this place in his journey to Buxar and hence this place bears the name Charanpuri.
