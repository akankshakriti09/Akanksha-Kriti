Chenari

Chenari is a small city, but there is a historical place in Chanari- Lord Shiva Temple named "Gupta Dham". There are 12 Panchayats in Chenari Block.otal area of Chenari is 257 km² including 253.68 km² rural area and 2.97 km² urban area. Chenari has a population of 1,31,528 peoples. There are 21,298 houses in the sub-district. There are about 151 villages in Chenari block.

Tourist Attractions in Chenari are: Gupteshwar Dham, Hills of Kaimur, Nawabgadh( Shergarh Fort) .
