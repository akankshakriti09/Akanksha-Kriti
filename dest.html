Rohtas district is one of the thirty-eight (38) districts of Bihar. It came into existence when Shahabad District was bifurcated into Bhojpur & Rohtas in 1972. Rohtas district is part of Patna division.Administrative headquarter of the district is Sasaram.

Rohtas District is comprises of 3 subdivisions namely Sasaram, Dehri-On-Sone & Bikramganj and 19 blocks:-

  • Akorhi Gola
  • Bikramganj
  • Chenari
  • Dawath
  • Dehri
  • Dinara
  • Karakat
  • Kargahar
  • Kochas
  • Nasriganj
  • Nauhatta
  • Nokha
  • Rajpur
  • Rohtas
  • Sanjhauli
  • Sasaram
  • Sheosagar
  • Suryapura
  • Tilouthu

The places which make Rohtas charming are:- Sasaram , Dehri-On-Sone , Dalmiyanagar , Rohtas , Tilouthu , Indrapuri , Bikramganj , Nasriganj , Chenari , Nauhatta .
