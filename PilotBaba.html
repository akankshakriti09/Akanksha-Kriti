Pilot Baba Mandir
Pic 1: Pilot Baba Dham (Somnath Temple)

Pilot Baba Mandir is situated in Udhopur village of Sasaram Block near S.P. Jain College. It is also known as Somnath Mandir. This temple is built by 'Pilot Baba' who is an Indian spiritual guru who was previously Wing Commander Kapil Singh, a fighter pilot with the Indian Air Force. Pilot Baba has established a number of Ashrams or spiritual centres both in India and abroad. Pilot baba temple consist of many indian gods : 80 ft Buddha Statue , 150 ft Shiva Statue etc.
