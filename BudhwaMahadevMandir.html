Budhwa Mahadev Mandir

Budhwa Mahadev Mandir is one of the oldest pilgrim place of Sasaram just eastward to tarachandi dham. It was related to Mahabharata period. It was established by Prashuram ji Maharajah. It is also known as Sonawgadh Shiv Mandir.
