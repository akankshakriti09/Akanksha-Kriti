Anand Kuti Dham (Payhari Baba Ka Ashram)
Pic 1: Anand Kuti Dham (Payhari Baba Ka Ashram)

Anand Kuti Dham is loacated in Tilouthu near Radha Shanta Mahavidyalaya. It is also known as 'Pahyari Baba Ka Ashram. This Ashram is situated in large area. There is very beautiful temple inside the Ashram in which women are not allowed. Also there is some peacock in Ashram premises.

Pic 2: Peacock in Ashram premises
Pic 3: Samadhi of Payhari Baba

Every year fair-festival is organized at the time of Kartik Purnima. Many devotees comes here from various places. A huge 'Bhandara' is offered to devotees. This fair is of 7 days. Bhajan - Kirtan & Hawan program held here continously at the time of Kartik purnima fair. In this fair many temporary stalls are made containing variety of food stalls, toys stalls, clothes, shoes, artifacts, utensils, kitchen equipments, household equipments etc. Children especially love fairs, as they get to buy different toys and play on different type of swings and ferry wheels.
