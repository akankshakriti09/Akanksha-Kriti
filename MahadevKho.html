Mahadev Khow Mandir
Pic 1: Mahadev Khow Mandir

Mahadev Khow Mandir is situated in Nauhatta block of Rohtas District. It is famous for the Shiva temple of 80 ft in height.The temple was damaged on the earthquake of 1934 was reconstructed by Raja Srinand Singh of Srinagar Estate. Mahadev Khoh is one of the best picnic spot you will find. A beautiful waterfall, lake or spring of water is favourite attractions of tourists. The natural scene here and the glory of Shiva Bhole Nath is immortal.

Pic 2: Waterfall in Mahadev Khow Mandir premises

The very famous Mela, known as Dashishanath Mela is organized near this temple in Bandu village of Nauhatta Block. Bandu is a small village, situated at the Sangam of rivers Sone, Koel and Sarswati (Sanaha).The village is blessed by Lord Dasasheeshanath Mahadeo, brought and annointed here from Mount Kailash, the abode of Lord Shiva by Sahashtrabahu.

Pic 3: Kaimur hills near Mahadev Khow Mandir

The village has untapped treasures of historical and religious legacies. Hindu epics, especially Skandh Puran, make vivid references to the village, Dashishanath Mahadeo and river Sone (Bhadrashila). Great sage Vishwamitra had his science laboratory nearby and had performed the miracle of bodily sending a living human being, King Trisanku to universe. This feat can be said to be a precursor to present day feat of landing men on the Moon.

Pic 4: Shivling in Mahadev Khow Mandir

At the Sangam point there is a very famous Shiva Linga Chabutra named Dashshishanath Mahadev Baba. Very nearest Shiva Linga is Baurahwa Shiv Ji at Amrkha Pahari. Baurahwa Shiv ji situated from thousand of years. In Bandu there are many Shiv Lingas.
