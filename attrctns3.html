Amra Talab is situated in small Village 'Amra' in Sasaram Block in Rohtas District of Bihar. It is most famous lake in this area which is famous for worship. Surya Mandir is located near this lake. Every year Chhath puja is celebrated by devotees in Amra Talab. A huge fair is organised in Amra Talab at the time of 'Jal Bhari Kalash Yatra' on the first day of Navratra. Lake is decorated with colourful ballons on the ocassion of Jal Bhari. Devotees comes to fill water in their kalash from Amra Talab in Navratra.

This Ancient Shadow Clock(Dhup Gadhi) is situated in Dehri-On-Sone near Sone River. This Shadow Clock is built by britishers in 1871. This Clock tells the time of day when there is sunlight by the apparent position of the Sun in the sky.This Clock is still working and used by the peoples who go by that way. This clock situated in 'Sinchai Yantrik Pramandal' (Irrigation Mechanical Division) of Dehri-On-sone is still used by locals to watch time.

Baba Satar Peer ka Mazar is peaceful and soothing place situated in Dehri-On-Sone on the bank of river Sone. It is believed that Mazar is very old and sacred. People go there with their wishes. It is believed that a person who prays with a pure heart at the Satar Peer Ka Mazar, gets all his or her wishes fulfilled. When the person wishes gets fullfilled they offer chadar(holy cloth) on Mazar. During the flood in river the place is at risk of submerging so refrain yourself from going at flood time.
