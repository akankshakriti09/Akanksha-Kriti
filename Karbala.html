Badi Karbala Saraiya
Pic 1: Badi Karbala of Saraiya

Badi Karbala is located in village Saraiya of Tilothu. This Karbala is more than 300 years old and situated on the bank of river Sone. It is one of the holiest place in Shi'a Islam. In the Battle of Karbala, the Prophet Muhammad's grandson Husayn was slain. To pay respects to the martyr Husayn, Shi'a muslims visit the Badi Karbala Saraiya, the biggest tourist attraction in Tilothu.

Pic 2: Mashk E Sakina

To mourn the martyrdom of Hazrat Imam Husain and his followers, the historical 72 taboot procession would be taken out here on sorrowful occasion of Muharram. With the slogans of 'Ya Ali! Ya Hussein !! Husseiniat Jindabads!' the procession was cleared from the Saraiya village. In this procession, thousands numbers of people reached the big Karbala, with wreaths and duldul, with weeds and navigations. This grand procession started by the Takia Ajakhana, reaching Karbala, Marakji Ajakhana, Janani Matkhana, Sultanbada, small Karbala. Where Husseini squads weaved down on the coals and at Saraiya Sadar Chowk Mod 'Zanjeeri Mantam' and 'Sinajani' is done by youths in the memory of Imam Hussein.
