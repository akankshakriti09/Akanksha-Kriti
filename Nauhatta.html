Nauhatta

Nauhatta is a Block situated in Rohtas district in Bihar. Positioned in rural region of Bihar, it is one of the 19 blocks of Rohtas district. According to the administration register, the block number of Nauhatta is 457. The block has 68 villages and there are total 16387 families in this Block. There are 11 Panchayats in Nauhatta Block.

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Nauhatta village is 252066. Nauhatta village is located in Nauhatta Tehsil of Rohtas district in Bihar, India. It is situated 90km away from district headquarter Nauhatta. Nauhatta is the sub-district headquarter of Nauhatta village. As per 2009 stats, Nauhatta village is also a gram panchayat.The latitude 24.9417928 and longitude 83.7285018 are the geocoordinate of the Nauhatta.

The total geographical area of village is 497 hectares. Nauhatta has a total population of 5,103 peoples. There are about 909 houses in Nauhatta village. Dehri is nearest town to Nauhatta which is approximately 65km away.

It is famous for the Shiva temple of 80 ft in height.The temple was damaged on the earthquake of 1934 was reconstructed by Raja Srinand Singh of Srinagar Estate. There is a grave of Madho Singh on an earthen mound about 40 ft high. Madho Sing had became a mortyr in the battle of Ladri Ghat.

Tourist attractions in Nauhatta are: Mahadev Kho Mandir , Shivling of Mahadev , Waterfall of Mahadev Kho , Mahadev Kho Tapasvi Ashram , Hills of Mahadev Kho.
