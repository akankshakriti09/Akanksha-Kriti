Bikramganj

Bikramganj is located at 25.2°N 84.25°E. It has an average elevation of 77 metres (252 feet). There are 12 Panchayats in Bikramganj Block.

The river Kaw passes from the middle of the city. The water of river Kaw is blessing for the villages surrounding Bikramganj because it is useful for agriculture.The main occupation of the people live here is agriculture.The land is very good for agriculture. The land is good for the production of wheat , rice, gram, vegetables e.t.c.

It is a small city however environment of education is very good. There are three good colleges and various good schools.Here some big markets are ready to stand for marketing or shoping. A.B.palace is already built for shopping.

Here is a famous temple called Ashkamini Mandir. It is called that the person who visited this temple get those thing which they needs. The Railway station is half KM away from this Mandir. There is also a famous temple called Bhaluni Bhavani 8 KM ( Natwar City) away from Bikramganj. It is Durga Mata mandir which is one of the oldest temple of India.

Tourist attractions in Bikramganj are: Bhaluni Bhavani Dham, Ashkamini Mandir, Dharupur Shiv mandir, Mauni Baba ki Kutiya, Durga Maa mandir , The origin of Thora river which starts from Nonhar.
