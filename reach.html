How to Reach

Best time to visit Rohtas district is in the months of winters or during post monsoon season, when weather remains pleasant. How to reach Rohtas district is not a major concern these days, as this place is well connected with major cities of the country through airways, railways and roadways. Nearest airport to Rohtas district is located in Gaya, at a distance of 85 Km from here.Nearest railway station to Rohtas district is the Dehri-On-Sone Station which is located at a distance of 85 km from Gaya by train & Sasaram Railway Station which is located at a distance of 136 km from Varanasi by train. Regular services of public and private buses connect the city with other cities of the state.

By Train

Sasaram is situated on Grand Chord Section (Howrah-Gaya-Delhi line) of Indian Railways. There are regular trains from other major cities of the country to Sasaram.

Major Railway Station(s): Sasaram Junction (SSM), Dehri-on-Sone (DOS), Bikramganj (XBKJ)

By Bus

Grand Trunk Road (NH-2) passes from Sasaram. Sasaram is well connected to major cities like Patna, Ara, Bilaspur, Varanasi, New Delhi, Kolkata, Bokaro, Jamshedpur etc. of the country via regular buses.

Bus Station(s): Situated at main town
