Jharkhandi Shiv Mandir
Pic 1: Jharkhandi Shiv Mandir

Jharkhandi Shiv Temple is a Shiva temple located in Dehri-on-Sone near old Anicut Dam on the bank of Sone river. It is one of the oldest temple of Dehri-On-Sone. Also Radhe Krishna Temple (Naulakha Mandir), Kali Mandir, Durga Mandir situated nearby this temple.

In the month of Shravaan, devotees of Lord Shiva on each Shomvari (Monday) pour pure water over the Shivling of this temple and keep reciting the mantra !!Om Namah Shivaya!!. Thereafter, place the sacred thread , Bel Patra , flowers and put the agarbatti (incense sticks). Offer sweets to the Lord and seek forgiving for the wrong deeds. Every year a well-organized fair is organized here on the occasion of 'Maha Shivratri' and 'Makar Sankranti'.

Pic 2: Shivling of Jharkhandi Shiv Mandir

During wedding season hundreds of couples from around the town and nearby villages gets married here. Best place for locals mostly used for the marriage ceremony and devotion.

Pic 3: Bells inside Jharkhandi Shiv Mandir
Pic 4: Inner area of Jharkhandi Shiv Mandir

Small parks is available here with playing material and jhulas near this temple. It is nice place to spend time in evening with friends and family too. It is also an favourite picnic spot for locals especially for new year celebration.
